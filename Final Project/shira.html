Background Information

Shirakami Fubuki (白上フブキ) is a female Japanese Virtual Youtuber associated with hololive, debuting as part of its first generation of VTubers alongside Yozora Mel, Aki Rosenthal, Natsuiro Matsuri and Akai Haato. She is also part of a unit inside hololive called hololive Gamers

Fubuki often becomes enthralled with the games she plays. During her time while playing games, she will often have conversations with scripted game characters or will even voice the characters while making various sound effects.

She greets her viewers with "Kon Kon Kitsune!," which official subs seem to interpret as "Foxy day to you!"

Fubuki is one to fearlessly march to the beat of her own drum, sometimes to a career risking degree. For example, she has once said that if hololive forced her to say something she didn't want to on stream, she would rather quit her job.

Timeline of Milestones
2018 Debuted with Hololive in May, then debuted her first 3D model later in October alongside Tokino Sora, Roboco, and Sakura Miko.
2019 In March, debuted her second 3D model. Announced there would be two new members of Hololive Gamers. Debuted her 3D swimsuit in August.
2020 In January, debuted her 2D costume of a kimono to celebrate the new year. Reached 1 million subscribers on Youtube and Bilibili.
2021 In July, reached 1,500,000 subscribers on Youtube during a Minecraft stream. Fubuki's Twitter account also became verified.
2022 Fubuki debuted her original song: "KINGWORLD." Debuted her 3.0 model. Reached 2 million subscribers on Youtube.
