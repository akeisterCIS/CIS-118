What is Hololive?

A Short Introduction

Hololive is a Virtual YouTuber agency owned by Japanese technology entertainment company Cover Corporation. It is a multi-channel network that also handles merchandising in music production and concert organization. As of April 2022, the agency manages 68 VTubers between four regional branches totalling over 43 million subscribers, including several of the most subscribed VTubers on YouTube.
