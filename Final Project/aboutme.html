So, Why is my Opinion Important?

To be perfectly honest, it really isn't in the grand scheme of things.

I've only just recently this year been introduced to Hololive and all of it's talents. A friend of mine introduced me to Hololive via short clips of their livestreams, and I found them funny. Additionally, a developer by the name of Kay Yu made the fangame "Holocure" which is a survival hack and slash game made with the characters and their movesets are largely based off of things related to the characters themselves. I spent many hours on this game and absolutely fell in love with it.

Thus, this kickstarted my likeness for Hololive.

I unfortunately do not have any photos with merchandise from the franchise, hwoever in the meantime I hope you enjoy this photo of me and my dog, Medic.

In the future, I intend to collect more Hololive merchandise, more specifically the figures made of these characters. Hopefully I can grow my collection to contain more than just figures, though.

To conclude this short blurb, I thoroughly enjoyed this project and I had a great time learning about the Hololive talents and the company itself. I hope to learn even more about them going forward exploring the franchise.
