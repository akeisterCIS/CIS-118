Background Information

Kobo Kanaeru (こぼ・かなえる) is a female Indonesian Virtual YouTuber associated with hololive, debuting as part of its Indonesian (ID) branch third generation of VTubers alongside Vestia Zeta and Kaela Kovalskia.

Because of her short stature and immature mannerisms, Kobo initially projects the image of an endearing, energetic child with strange tendencies. However, she more often acts like a sassy, spoiled brat who frequently demands praise and playfully insults her peers or banters with them. She can very quickly lose her temper when enraged or provoked (notably by Regis Altare), and also gets flustered easily.

She is often considered one of the most "childlike" members of hololive that inspires others to pamper her like their little sister or daughter, although she is not above taking advantage of this to gain (often material) benefits. In spite of her childish "gremlin-like" attitude, not only is she tremendously talented and clever, but also capable of genuine sweetness when expressing her gratitude and happiness.

Although her somewhat clumsy English tends to project the image of an innocent, clueless child, those who understand her talking in Indonesian tend to understand her bratty mannerisms and language more. However, as she got more used to talking with her fellow members, the gap between the two has diminished

Timeline of Milestones
2021 In March, Hololive Indonesia debuted their third generation. Her debut stream received a peak viewership of 46,922. It featured an elaborate presentation designed in the form of a corporate website for her business as a rain shaman. It also featured a preview of Kobo's original song Mantra Hujan ("Rain Spell"), which she composed, wrote, sang, and created the music video for herself.
2022 In September, she reached 1,500,000 Youtube Subscribers. This makes her the 13th Hololive member to do so, as well as the first member of the Indonesian branch to do so.s
